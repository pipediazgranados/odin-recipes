Kung Pao Chicken

Home

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 2 small boneless chicken breasts (~340g), cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 2 teaspoons light soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon Shaoxing wine
  • 1 large pinch kosher salt

For the Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons Chinkiang Vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon Shaoxing Wine
  • 2 teaspoons light soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water or chicken stock

For the Stir Fry:

  • 3 tablespoons veg oil
  • 6-12 samll dried red chilies cut into 1/2-inch pieces
  • 1 teaspoon sichuan peppercorns
  • 4 medium cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1-inch knob ginger grated
  • 6 medium scallions, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
  • 3/4 cup roasted peanuts

Steps:

  1. For the Chicken: Combine chicken ingredients in a small bowl and turn until well mixed and chicken is evenly coated in a thing film of the cornstarch paste. Set asiede.
  2. For the Sauce: Combine sauce ingredients in a small bowl and stri with a fork until no clumps of cornstarch remain
  3. To Stir Fry: Pour a small amount of oil into a large wok and rub around. Place over high heat until smoking
  4. Add remaining oil, chilies and peppercorns. Stir-fry until fragrant but not burnt. Immediately add chicken and stir-fry until there are no pink spots on the exterior
  5. Add garlic and ginger, stir-fry until fragrant. Add scalions and peanuts, stir-fry for 30 seconds
  6. Add sauce and stir-fry until coated evenly and chicken is cooked through. Add water 1 tablespoon at a time if necessary to keep sauce from clumping
  7. Serve with white rice and enjoy!
