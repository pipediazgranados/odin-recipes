College Style Ramen

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 pack cheap ramen
  • 2 green onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 clove garlic, finely minced
  • 1 tablespoon lao gan ma, or chilli oil of choice

Steps

  1. Pour ramen powder into water and prepare ramen following instructions on the back of the pack, save some of the water after finishing cooking
  2. Mix lao gan ma, garlic and peanut butter in a small bowl
  3. Pour noodles and mix
  4. Pour in leftover ramen stock until sauce is homogenous
  5. Garnish with green onion and enjoy!
